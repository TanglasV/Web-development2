History

Bruno Pizzaria was established by Alfred Bruno in 1971. He was one of the finest chef of radiator Springs. His intention was to bring the taste of original pizza to the small town of Radiator Springs and thus the journey of Bruno Pizzaria began. The business has been run by his family for past 3 generations and continues to serve delicious pizza for the townsfolk and tourists as well.

Location

Bruno Pizzaria is located at Radiator Springs, Carburetor Country, Arizona, United States of America. Grab your pack & get over here to experience the dazzling beauty of deserts, and the deliciousness of real italian pizza!
